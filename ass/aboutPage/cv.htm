Personal statement

Rose (http://rosegoldthorp.com) is an energetic, motivated, and engaged young woman, who has just returned, with her family, to London, from New Zealand (after graduating from Auckland University). She is enthusiastically looking forward to all of the opportunities she can grasp. Rose is exceptionally good at operating and organising fast paced, challenging and changing environments.
