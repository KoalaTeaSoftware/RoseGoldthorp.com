Example locations for the feature film Silverville

Couldrey House

Couldrey House is an historic Victorian-Edwardian family home set in parkland. It is fully furnished but in a mix of styles reflecting the eras and preoccupations of some notable past owners.

Couldrey House is in the Wenderholm Regional Park, so is easy to find

Silverdale Pioneer Village

The Silverdale & Districts Historical Society's Pioneer Village preserves some examples of the domestic architecture of the area.

The village is in Silverdale, at 15 Wainui Road, Silverdale .

Hellensville Pioneer Museum

Helensville & District Historical Society preserves and promotes the history of South Kaipara.

You can find the Helensville Museum is on the right just as you leave the Helensville. Approx 30 minutes from Orewa.
